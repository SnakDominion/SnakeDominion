HOW TO PLAY

🎮 CONTROLS
🖱️ MouseSteer your snake
⎵ SpaceBoost (drains length)
⏸ EscapePause game
📱 JoystickMove (mobile)
🔴 BOOST btnBoost (mobile)
🐍 OBJECTIVE

Collect food (mice, frogs, eggs & rats) to grow longer. Avoid crashing into other snakes' bodies — if your head touches their body, you're eliminated! Cut off enemies to make them crash into you.

⚡ BOOST

Hold Boost to move faster — but it slowly drains your length. Use it to escape danger or intercept enemies. You cannot boost if your length is 10 or below.

🏆 TIPS
  • Bigger snakes move slower — stay small to dodge easily.
  • Circle smaller snakes to trap and eliminate them.
  • Watch the mini-map to track nearby threats.
  • Boost briefly to steal an orb before an enemy reaches it.
